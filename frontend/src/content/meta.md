## How This Site Works

### The AI
This site uses an AI assistant powered by Gemini to help you explore my experience interactively. You can ask questions about my work, skills, projects, or anything else on my resume. The AI has access to all my content and responds conversationally.

### The Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **AI:** Google Gemini 2.5 Flash (streaming responses)
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **Content:** Markdown files with story-based structure

### Privacy & Data
- No user data is stored permanently
- Chat conversations exist only in your browser session
- No cookies beyond theme preference
- The AI only knows what's in my resume content
